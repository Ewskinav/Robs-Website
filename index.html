Hi I'm Robbie, I'm an Artist & Illustrator based in Co. Antrim, Northern Ireland. My illustration work employs a blend of digital and traditional methods to create angular & playful imagery. My process is often very spontaneous and instinctual when pursuing ideas, using a variety of traditional mediums that I edit digitally. My practice has always been at the core of who I am, always thinking visually and creatively with the intent to have my voice and personality shine through. I am eager to work in the fields of character design, concept art and illustrative work but I am always open and enthusiastic about new opportunities to branch out! Currently studying in my final year of Graphic Design & Illustration; I'm looking forward to kickstarting my career in 2026.
